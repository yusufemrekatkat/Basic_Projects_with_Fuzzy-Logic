## üß† Core Methodology
As a **Mathematical Engineer**, I designed the logic to replicate the intuition of a seasoned Market Strategist.

### 1. Relative Pricing Logic
Unlike standard models that look at absolute prices, this engine evaluates the **Competitor Gap (%)**. 
* *Insight:* If a competitor is $50\%$ cheaper, the model triggers a "Survival Mode" (Slash Prices). However, if the gap is small ($-5\%$), it holds the line.

### 2. The "Loyalty Shield" (4th Dimension)
In the final stage, we introduced **Loyalty** as a mathematical dampener.
* *The Apple Effect:* Even when the Competitor Gap is negative (Cheaper), high loyalty inputs prevent the price from dropping. The logic shifts from "Competition" to "Dominance."

* **Margin Protection:** Prevents algorithmic panic. The system refuses to lower prices for loyal customers just because a competitor is discounting.
